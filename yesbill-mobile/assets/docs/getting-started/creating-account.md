# Creating Your Account

YesBill uses secure email-based authentication. You can register with your email and password, or sign in with Google.

## Sign Up

![Create account screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Create-account-screen-01.jpeg)

1. Open the YesBill app and tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your **full name**, **email address**, and a strong **password**.
3. Tap **Sign Up**.
4. Check your inbox for a confirmation email and tap the verification link.

## Sign In

![Login screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/login-screen-01.jpeg)

1. Open YesBill and enter your registered **email** and **password**.
2. Tap **Sign In**.
3. Or tap **Continue with Google** for one-tap sign-in.

## Forgot Password

![Forgot password screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Forget-Password-screen-01.jpeg)

1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your registered email address and tap **Send Reset Link**.
3. Open the password reset email in your inbox and follow the link to set a new password.
4. Return to the app and sign in with your new credentials.

:::info
Password reset links expire after 1 hour for security reasons. Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
:::

## Other Login Issues

- **Email not verified?** Check your spam folder for the original confirmation email.
- **Forgot your password?** Use the **Forgot Password?** link on the login screen.
- **Need help?** See [Support](/settings/support).

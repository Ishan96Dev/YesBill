# Profile Settings

Manage your personal information and profile photo from the Profile section in Settings.

## Editing Your Profile

![Profile and edit profile screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Profile-Edit-Profile-Screen-01.jpeg)

1. Tap your avatar or name at the top of the screen.
2. Tap **Edit Profile**.
3. Update your name, display name, phone number, or bio.
4. Tap **Save** to confirm changes.

## What You Can Edit

- **Full Name** — Your preferred name
- **Display Name** — Short name shown in greetings
- **Phone Number** — Optional
- **Bio** — Up to 500 characters

:::tip
Add a recognisable profile photo — it makes the app feel more personal.
:::

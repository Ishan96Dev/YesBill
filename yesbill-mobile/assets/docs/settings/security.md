# Security Settings

Manage your account security from the Security section in Settings.

## Security Settings Screen

![Security screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Security-Screen-01.jpeg)

Tap **Settings** → **Security** to access security options.

## Changing Your Password

1. Tap **Change Password**.
2. Enter your **Current Password**.
3. Enter and confirm your **New Password**.
4. Tap **Update Password**.

Password requirements: at least 8 characters, with uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a symbol.

:::warning
After changing your password, all other active sessions will be signed out automatically.
:::

## Deleting Your Account

:::danger
Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All your services, tracking data, and bills will be deleted and cannot be recovered.
:::

![Delete account screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Delete-Acoount-Screen-01.jpeg)

Scroll to the bottom of the Security screen and tap **Delete Account**.

![Delete account confirmation screen](/img/screenshots/mobile/Delete-Account-Confirmation-Screen-01.jpeg)

A confirmation screen appears. Type your account email address to confirm, then tap **Delete**.

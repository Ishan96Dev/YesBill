# Security Settings

Manage your account security, including password changes, email change, sign-out of all sessions, and account deletion.

## Security Screen

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Security-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Security settings screen" />

Open **Settings → Security** to access all security options.

## Changing Your Password

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Security-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Security settings — change password" />

1. Tap **Change Password**.
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter and confirm your **new password** (minimum 8 characters).
4. Tap **Update Password**.

![Change password from Settings](/img/screenshots/Change-Password-From-Settings-01.png)

### Password Changed Email

![Password changed confirmation email](/img/screenshots/Password-Changed-Mail-After-Changing-Password-01.png)

After a successful password change, YesBill sends a confirmation email to your registered address. If you didn't request this change, contact support immediately.

## Current Session

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Security-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Security screen with session info" />

The Security screen shows your **current active session** details:
- Last sign-in time and date
- Device type and OS
- IP address (approximate location)

This helps you verify that no unauthorised access has occurred.

## Delete Account

:::danger Irreversible action
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data — services, bills, calendar records, and AI conversations — will be erased immediately.
:::

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Delete-Acoount-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Delete account screen" fit="contain" bg="#f8f9ff" />

![Delete Account from Settings](/img/screenshots/Delete-Account-From-Settings-01.png)

1. Tap **Delete Account** at the bottom of the Security screen.
2. Read the full warning about what will be permanently deleted.

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Delete-Account-Confirmation-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Delete account confirmation" />

![Delete account confirmation from Settings](/img/screenshots/Delete-account-Confirmation-From-Settings-01.png)

3. Type your **email address** in the confirmation field to prove intent.
4. Tap **Permanently Delete Account**.

Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

### Account Deletion Email

![Account deleted confirmation email](/img/screenshots/Account-Delete-Mail-01.png)

A final confirmation email is sent to your address once deletion is complete.

## Account Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password (at least 12 characters with symbols)
- Sign out on devices you don't own — see Settings → Sign Out
- Review your active session periodically from Security settings
- Never share your API keys or password with anyone

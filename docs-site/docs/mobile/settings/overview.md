# Settings Overview

The **Settings** screen gives you control over your account, notifications, appearance, AI configuration, and more.

## Accessing Settings

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Settings-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Settings screen" />

Tap the **profile icon** (top-right of the Home screen) or navigate to Settings via the menu. The Settings screen is organized into sections:

- **Account** — Profile, email, password
- **Notifications** — Push notifications and alerts
- **Appearance** — Dark/light mode, theme
- **AI Configuration** — Provider, API key, model
- **Security** — Password, delete account
- **Support** — Help, feedback, about

## Sign Out

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Sign-out-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Sign out confirmation screen" fit="contain" bg="#1a1a2e" />

To sign out:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Sign Out**.
3. Confirm in the dialog.

You'll be taken back to the login screen. Your data remains saved in the cloud and will be available when you sign back in.

:::info
Signing out does not delete your data. To permanently delete your account and all data, see [Security Settings](/mobile/settings/security).
:::

# Appearance Settings

Customise how YesBill looks on your device.

## Appearance Screen

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Appearance-Settings-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Appearance settings screen" />

Open **Settings → Appearance** to access theme and display options.

## Theme

Choose how YesBill's interface appears:

| Option | Behaviour |
|--------|-----------|
| **Light** | Always uses the light colour scheme |
| **Dark** | Always uses the dark colour scheme |
| **System Default** | Follows your device's system-wide dark/light setting |

Tap your preferred option and the app switches immediately — no restart needed.

## Dark Mode Preview

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Dark-Mode-Dashboard-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Dashboard in dark mode" />

## Tips

- **System Default** is recommended for most users — the app switches automatically at sunrise/sunset if your device uses automatic scheduling.
- Dark mode reduces battery usage on OLED screens (common on modern Android and iPhone displays).
- All screenshots and bill exports always use the light scheme regardless of your theme preference.

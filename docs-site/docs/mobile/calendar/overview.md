# Calendar Overview

The **Calendar** tab is where you mark daily deliveries and visits. YesBill uses these marks to calculate your monthly bills.

## Calendar View

<PhoneFrame src="/img/screenshots/mobile/Calender-Screen-01.jpeg" alt="Calendar screen" />

The calendar shows the current month with a grid of days. For each service, you can mark days as delivered, missed, or not applicable.

## Marking a Delivery or Visit

1. Tap **Calendar** in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Tap on a **date** to open the day view.
3. For each service listed, tap the checkmark to mark it as delivered/visited.
4. Tap again to unmark if you made a mistake.

## Color Codes

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Green | Delivery or visit marked |
| Red | Marked as absent/missed |
| Grey | Not marked (no record) |

## Switching Between Services

If you have multiple services, swipe left/right or use the service selector at the top of the calendar to switch between them.

## Retroactive Marking

You can mark past dates — tap any previous day in the calendar to update its record. This is useful if you forgot to mark deliveries for a few days.

:::info
Calendar marks feed directly into bill generation. Make sure all marks for the month are complete before generating your monthly bill.
:::

## Yearly View

Tap the **year icon** (top-right of the calendar) to switch to a yearly overview, showing which months have complete records and which have gaps.
